About Me

My name is Yueting Han. I just finished my undergraduate study of Mathematics at University of Liverpool, UK. I am soon going to undertake MathSys CDT program at University of Warwick, UK.

Although my major is Mathematics, I focused more on statistics and data science in my final year of undergraduate study. During this time, my study was mostly based on their theory, relevant proof and mathematical programming. In addition, I had experience of working as a research assistant in Tsinghua Univeristy, where I processed big data and devised algorithm for projects about Computational Fluid Dynamics.
